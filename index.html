About ZENITHACK

Empowering Innovation Through Technology

What is ZENITHACK?

ZENITHACK is a national-level 48-hour hackathon that brings together visionary minds to solve real-world problems through cutting-edge technology, collaboration, and creative thinking. It's a platform where innovation meets execution, and ideas evolve into impactful prototypes.

Our Mission

To inspire and nurture innovation by providing an inclusive and collaborative environment for students, developers, and creators to build solutions that address real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To become one of India’s most forward-thinking student-led hackathons — pushing boundaries in technology, cultivating creativity, and empowering the next generation of change-makers.

❓ FAQs ❓

▶️ What is the registration fee?

₹180 per person.

▶️ Who can participate?

Any college student from any domain or year can register and participate.

▶️ Can we participate as a team?

Yes, teams of 2 to 4 members are allowed.

▶️ Will there be certificates?

Yes, all participants who got selected frist round they will receive certificates.

▶️ Is it an online or offline hackathon?

It is completely online across all rounds.

▶️ Is there any prize?

Yes, of course! The total prize pool is ₹3,00,000 🎉

▶️ Is the registration fee refundable if not selected?

No, the registration fee is non-refundable.

▶️ If I have any doubts, where should I ask?

You can reach out to us anytime at nfrist35@gmail.com. We're here to help!

▶️ What is the selection process?

There are 3 rounds:
1️⃣ Idea Submission (fill during payment),
2️⃣ Prototype Round (for shortlisted teams),
3️⃣ Final Round.
Only selected teams from the idea submission will receive confirmation via Gmail.

▶️ What is the last date for registration?

The last date to pay the registration fee and submit your idea is 18-06-2025.

▶️ I didn’t get any confirmation mail. What does that mean?

If you haven’t received a confirmation mail, it means your idea was not selected. Don’t feel disheartened – every great innovator starts somewhere! Keep trying and come back stronger with a more unique and impactful idea next time. We believe in you! 💡💪
